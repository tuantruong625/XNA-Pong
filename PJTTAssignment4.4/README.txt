Game Specifications

Left Player's Paddle will be controlled by A and Z keys
Right Player's Paddle will be conntrolled by Up and Down Keys

Ball will be stationnary at the begining of each turn, press enter to release the ball
Ball will have random speeds between 3,9 and directions

Max Score to win is 2, Game will show winner's name

ESC key will exit the game.

Space bar will reset the score and previous winner's name and paddle position.
